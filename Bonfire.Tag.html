    Bonfire.Tag (Bonfire v1.0.0-social-rc.1.21)

    View Source

    An extension for Bonfire that handles tagging posts or other things:

    • @ mentions
    • #hashtags
    • others (e.g. with a Category from Bonfire.Classify or a location from Bonfire.Geolocate)
