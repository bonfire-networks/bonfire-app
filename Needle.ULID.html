    Needle.ULID (Bonfire v1.0.0-social-rc.1.25)

    View Source

    hexdocs

    A ULID datatype and some postgres support for ULID operations (min and max functions and aggregates)

    Originally forked from ecto-ulid but the internals have been replaced with a dependency on ex_ulid
